Election Guidelines

AAA Officers Election AY 2026–2027

  1. Only registered AAA members with a valid Student ID and school email (@sjp2cd.edu.ph) are eligible to vote.
  2. Each voter may cast their vote only once. Duplicate voting is strictly prohibited.
  3. Voters must select one candidate per position. Incomplete ballots will not be accepted.
  4. Once submitted, votes are final and cannot be changed.
  5. Campaigning, vote-buying, and any form of electoral fraud are strictly prohibited.
  6. All personal data collected is used solely for voter verification and will not be shared with third parties.
  7. Results will be announced by the Election Committee after the polls close on May 6, 2026 at 11:59 PM.
  8. Any disputes or concerns must be raised with the Election Committee within 24 hours after the polls close.
